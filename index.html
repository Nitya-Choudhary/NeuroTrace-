About NeuroTrace

NeuroTrace is an advanced machine learning model designed for the detection and classification of brain tumors from MRI scans. Leveraging deep learning techniques, NeuroTrace aims to assist radiologists and medical professionals in identifying brain tumors with high accuracy, enabling early diagnosis and improved patient outcomes.

  • High Accuracy Detection: Uses CNNs to detect abnormalities with precision.
  • Tumor Classification: Distinguishes between glioma, meningioma, and pituitary tumors.
  • Multi-Modal Support: Works on T1, T2, and FLAIR MRI sequences.
  • Real-Time Processing: Fast and optimized for clinical use.
  • Explainability: Attention mechanisms show important image areas.
